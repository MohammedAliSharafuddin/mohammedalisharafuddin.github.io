04 · Software

Survey & statistics software

surveyframe is live on CRAN, an R package for end-to-end survey research workflows. StatChooser is its free companion web tool: answer four short questions and get a recommended statistical test with assumptions, runnable R code, JASP steps, and a ready survey skeleton. The next FlairMI software line is in active testing: QuartoPad focuses on Quarto authoring, TrimWrite on revision and voice cleanup, and Ethos on survey workflow from instrument design to reproducible reporting.

Live

surveyframe

On CRAN

An R package for end-to-end survey research workflows: visual instrument design via SurveyBuilder or SurveyStudio, static HTML survey export, Shiny module, response quality checking, scale scoring, psychometric diagnostics, analysis-plan execution, codebook generation, and reproducible HTML reporting. Powered by the .sframe format with SHA-256 integrity checking.

RCRANSurveyPsychometricsQuestionnaire
install.packages("surveyframe")

StatChooser

Live

A free, static web tool that recommends a statistical test from four short questions about your research goal, design, data, and measurement level. Every recommendation includes the test assumptions, runnable surveyframe R code, JASP steps, an APA reporting sentence, a small-sample note, and a ready-to-adopt survey skeleton. Companion front end to the surveyframe R package.
