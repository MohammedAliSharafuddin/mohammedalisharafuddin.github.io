Live Surveys

Active research survey instruments open to eligible participants. Each survey is built with surveyframe and deployed here as a self-contained static instrument. Responses are collected anonymously via Google Sheets. The instrument object, analysis plan, and structural model are declared before data collection.

01 · Open Now

AIC-RSAM Pilot Study

Open

A pre-registered acceptance test of an AI-assisted conversational room-service booking system in Maldivian resorts. Participants complete a simulated booking prototype, then answer a post-use questionnaire covering six reflective constructs: Perceived AI Assistance, Perceived Order Assurance, Perceived Ease of Use, Perceived Usefulness, Perceived Risk, and Behavioural Intention to Use.

Model: AIC-RSAM (PLS-SEM, 9 paths) Tool: surveyframe 0.3.2 Conference: ICSRI 2026, Villa College

Eligibility: Maldivian nationals or residents, aged 18 or above, who have stayed in a Maldivian resort within the past 12 months for at least 2 nights.

02 · About These Surveys

All instruments on this page are built with the surveyframe R package and exported as single self-contained HTML files. Each file runs entirely in the browser with no server dependency. Responses are collected via a Google Apps Script endpoint and a local CSV download fires on every submission as a backup. The instrument definition, hypotheses, and analysis plan are locked before data collection begins.
